Cleanup and Catch-Up

Your books are wrong. Let's fix them properly.

Scoped on a call

Bad books are not just messy. They are costing you real money in bad decisions, missed deductions, and conversations with your CPA that go nowhere because the numbers cannot be trusted.

We clean up the mess and give you books that make sense again.

We go in, reconcile everything, correct what is wrong, and hand you back a file that actually reflects your business. Most cleanups take 4 to 6 weeks per year of history.

When we are done, you will have a clear financial picture, a summary of what changed, and a foundation you can actually build on.

What's included

  • Review of your current QuickBooks file
  • Bank and credit card reconciliations
  • Cleanup of incorrect or uncategorized transactions
  • Balance sheet review
  • Loan and credit card balance review when statements are provided
  • Clean reports for the periods included
  • Handoff summary explaining what changed

Best for

Business owners who are months or years behind, do not trust their reports, or need clean books for tax filing, financing, or better decisions.
