About KeepBookers

At KeepBookers, we believe that clarity changes everything.

Most business owners are not avoiding their finances because they do not care. They are avoiding them because no one ever made the numbers feel safe, approachable, or worth looking at. So they look away. They hope things will work themselves out. And in the meantime, the opportunities that were always there go unnoticed.

We exist to change that.

KeepBookers is a bookkeeping and advisory firm built for small business owners who are ready to stop guessing and start making confident decisions. We keep your books clean, your reports accurate, and your finances visible. And then we help you understand what you are actually looking at.

About Our Founder

Elena Kritopulos founded KeepBookers with one goal in mind: to give small business owners the financial clarity they need to run their businesses with confidence.

Elena has a background in finance and a deep belief that good financial management is not reserved for big companies with big budgets. Small businesses deserve the same level of care, the same quality of insight, and the same steady support. KeepBookers was built to provide exactly that.

What sets Elena apart is not just her expertise. It is how she works with people. She has seen firsthand how much changes for a business owner when the numbers finally make sense. When the anxiety around money is replaced with understanding. When decisions that used to feel overwhelming start to feel clear. That transformation is what drives everything she does.
